ClipFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Simple Promise: ClipFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your Mac.

Information We Don't Collect

ClipFlow is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Your clipboard content or history
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Device information
  • Any form of user data

How ClipFlow Works

ClipFlow operates entirely on your local Mac:

  • Local Storage: All clipboard history is stored locally on your device using standard macOS storage
  • No Network Access: ClipFlow works completely offline and makes no network connections
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your Mac
  • No External Services: We don't use any third-party analytics or tracking services

Data Security

Your clipboard data security:

  • Stored using macOS standard security practices
  • Protected by your Mac's user account permissions
  • Never transmitted over the internet
  • Automatically cleaned up based on your preferences

App Permissions

ClipFlow requests minimal permissions:

  • Clipboard Access: Required to monitor and manage clipboard content
  • File System Access: Only for saving user preferences locally

Data Retention

You have full control over your data:

  • Set maximum number of clipboard items to store
  • Clear clipboard history anytime from settings
  • Option to clear history when quitting the app
  • Uninstalling ClipFlow removes all stored data

Third-Party Services

ClipFlow does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external platforms. The app operates in complete isolation on your Mac.

Children's Privacy

ClipFlow does not collect any data, making it safe for users of all ages. No special provisions are needed for children's privacy as no data collection occurs.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted here and in the app. Given that ClipFlow doesn't collect data, privacy policy changes will be minimal and focused on clarification rather than new data practices.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@clipflow.app
  • GitHub: github.com/yourusername/clipflow

Compliance

ClipFlow's privacy-first design ensures compliance with:

  • Apple's App Store privacy requirements
  • GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Other international privacy regulations
Bottom Line: ClipFlow is built to enhance your productivity while respecting your privacy completely. Your clipboard data belongs to you and stays with you.
